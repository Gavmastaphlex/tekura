    Your teachers

    Help is only an email or a free phone call away at Te Kura. If you are a full-time or fee-paying student, or in some cases, a young adult [Definition] student, you will be assigned a learning advisor (what’s known as a form teacher, class teacher or whānau teacher in other schools) as soon as you’ve enrolled.

    If you need extra support, you may be assigned a liaison teacher [Definition] as well.

    Many students from Year 9 and up will also have subject teachers.

    If you have a learning advisor, you should keep in touch with them regularly. Contact them or your subject teacher whenever you have a question or need some help with your schoolwork.

    Getting hold of your learning advisor or teacher

    You can call your learning advisor or teacher free on 0800 65 99 88. If you know their extension number you can then dial it to go straight through to them, or dial ‘0’ and ask the receptionist to put you through.

    Because we have so many students, it really helps when you have your student ID number handy. If you get the teacher’s voicemail, please leave them a message with your name, student ID number and contact phone number. They will call you back as soon as they can.

    You can also email your learning advisor or teachers – our standard email address format is firstname.surname@tekura.school.nz.

    When to contact your learning advisor or teacher

    Contact your learning advisor or teacher if you have a question or a problem with your school work, or if:

    • you are running out of work or don’t know what to do next
    • you receive the wrong resources or something is missing
    • the schoolwork is too easy or too difficult
    • medical or family circumstances are affecting your schoolwork
    • you are having difficulties with online access
    • your home address, phone number or email address has changed.
