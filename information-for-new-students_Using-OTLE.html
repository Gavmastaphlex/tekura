    Using OTLE

    If you’re learning online you’ll be spending lots of time on OTLE. OTLE is short for ‘Online Teaching and Learning Environment’. It’s the place where you access your work, participate in discussions, do quizzes to test your learning, communicate with your teacher and more.

    Working online means you can access your learning resources wherever and whenever you need them. What’s more, you can work at your own pace and get feedback quickly from your teacher.

    If you’re new to OTLE, we recommend you watch this short video – don’t worry, you’ll find your way around in no time!

    Top tips

    Logging in to OTLE

    You can log in by hovering over the link at the top right-hand corner of our homepage.

    Username and password

    Use your student ID number as both your username and password the first time you access OTLE. Once you log in you’ll be asked to set a new password.

    Browser to use

    Log in to OTLE using Google Chrome, Firefox or Safari. Please don’t use Internet Explorer as it will not display everything you need.

    Changing your password/forgotten passwords

    If you have forgotten your password or want to change it, click on the ‘forgot password’ link at the bottom of the login screen. A password re-set link will be sent to the email address you have provided to us.

    Learning how to use OTLE

    After you log in for the first time, you should complete the introductory course called ‘OTLE HUB’. This will help you get up to speed really quickly.

    Contacting the OTLE Helpdesk

    If you have any issues with using OTLE contact the OTLE Helpdesk at helpdesk.OTLE@tekura.school.nz or phone 0800 65 99 88 ext 8712 between 8am and 5pm Monday to Friday. Be sure to provide your name and student ID number.
