    Tertiary Link

    Have you got your eye on a particular career and want a head start? We offer five tertiary-based, practical subjects outside the normal school curriculum.

    Students in years 12 and above can apply to study the following subjects:

    • Early Childcare
    • Tourism
    • Farming
    • Automotive and Car Ownership
    • Equine

    In addition, year 11 students can try basic Car Ownership and Maintenance papers.

    How does it work?

    Tertiary Link courses are mostly theoretical but may include some practical work. They will give you an idea of what it’s like to study at tertiary level – and may help you decide whether a certain career path is right for you.

    Who can apply?

    Tertiary Link courses are available to senior students – either full-time students or young adults enrolled in three or more subjects.

    Each subject has different criteria so read the information sheets above to see whether you are eligible to apply. If you are interested in enrolling, have a chat with your learning advisor to see whether they think a Tertiary Link course would suit you.

    If accepted, your fees will be covered by Te Kura.

    Learn more

    Each subject has a different contact person. See our subject information sheets above to find out who to talk to.
