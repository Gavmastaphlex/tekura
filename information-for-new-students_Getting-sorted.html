    Getting sorted

    Welcome to Te Kura! You are now part of one of the biggest schools worldwide.

    While aspects of learning with Te Kura are similar to what happens at face-to-face schools, there are a few differences. Get sorted by learning how it all works and connect to your Te Kura community.

    Where to start

    Many of our courses are delivered through our Online Teaching and Learning Environment (OTLE). To get started, login to OTLE using your Te Kura student ID number as your user name and password.

    See our computer requirements page for full information about the type of equipment you need.

    If you don’t have a computer, you may be eligible to apply for laptop and internet assistance.

    Te Kura students can download Microsoft software for free.

    In addition to the online tools and resources in OTLE, we provide a range of teaching and support materials. Depending on what you’re studying and the level you’re at, this can include:

    • printed booklets
    • workbooks
    • readers
    • audio resources
    • CDs and DVDs
    • an MP3 recorder
    • interactive CD-ROMs
    • textbooks
    • mathematics and science boxes
    • art packs
    • craft materials for technology.

    Special education and early childhood students may also receive puzzles, games or activities.

    What you’ll need

    • Your own stationery (notebooks, paper, pens, pencils etc.).
    • Access to a computer and the internet [Link TBC] (essential for online courses, highly desirable for other courses).

    You might have to supply your own materials or equipment for some courses. Check out our course page to find out what you need or talk to your learning advisor or teacher.
