    Service Level Agreement

    Our Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the responsibilities of Te Kura and of the dual student’s school of enrolment.

    The SLA explains:

    • teaching, curriculum materials and assessment service responsibilities of Te Kura
    • the school of enrolment’s responsibilities for the student’s attendance and supervision and for meeting Te Kura course completion and return of work requirements
    • the circumstances in which Te Kura is required by its funding arrangements and by its enrolment policy to terminate dual tuition.

    There are two versions of the SLA:

    Service Level Agreement for dual students (this SLA applies to all other dual providers)

    Service Level Agreement for Regional Health Schools

    Accepting the terms and conditions of the SLA

    By registering one or more students with Te Kura for dual tuition, you are accepting the terms and conditions of the SLA.

    Your Principal, Principal’s Nominee, School Coordinator and any teacher aides working with special education students should all have copies of the SLA for reference. Please note the specific conditions about qualifications in the last section.

    More information

    Dual tuition entry criteria or a dual student’s registration

    Email enrolment@tekura.school.nz or phone 0800 65 99 88 (select option 1 from the menu).

    Student’s course or progress

    Call or email the Te Kura subject teacher. The Te Kura Enrolment System (TES) lists the teachers for each dual student and their contact details.

    SLA questions or other

    Contact the Relationship Coordinator for your region.
