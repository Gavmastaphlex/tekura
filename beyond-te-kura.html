Beyond Te Kura

Beyond Te Kura

Thanks for studying with Te Kura! We hope you’ve enjoyed learning with us. If you are finishing your studies, please make sure we know your plans.

There’s just a bit of housekeeping you need to do before finishing up.

Notification of withdrawal

If you have a learning advisor, they will work with you to organise what you need to do when you finish studying with Te Kura. Adult or fee-paying students should contact our Enrolment Services team to notify them. Call 0800 65 99 88 and select option 1 from the menu, or email enrolment@tekura.school.nz.

Pack up and return all loan materials

Mark parcels ‘Attention [your teacher’s name]’. Include your name, address and student ID number in and on all packs of materials.

If you’re in New Zealand you can send parcels by freepost.

Send them by air or surface mail if you live overseas – note that you will need to pay the postage.

If you’re going to a new school let us know where

If you’re going to a new school, tell your learning advisor or teacher the name of your new school as soon as possible. Tell your new school that you have been attending Te Kura (formerly The Correspondence School).
