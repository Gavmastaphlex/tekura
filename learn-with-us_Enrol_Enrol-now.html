    Enrol now

    So you’ve worked out that you’re eligible to enrol and you’re keen to get started. Good for you!

    When filling out our form the first thing you need to do is select which type of student you are. If you get stuck, read about the enrolment types (note: if you are enrolling as a ‘Young adult’, use the ‘Adult’ link).

    You can enrol online or download [CONFIRM - Separate page to display forms?] an enrolment form and post the completed form to us.

    We aim to process enrolment applications within 10 to 15 working days. This can vary depending on the time of year.

    If you’re doing an online course, you will receive an email with login instructions as soon as your enrolment has been approved so that you can start working straight away.

    Enrolment types

    Early childhood

    An early childhood student is aged between 3–6 years old, and is a New Zealand citizen, permanent resident or a domestic student who meets one of the eligibility criteria set out by the Ministry of Education. Students aged under 3 will be put on the ECE wait list.[Glossary]

    Full-time

    A full-time student is aged between 5–19 years old, residing in New Zealand and is a New Zealand citizen, permanent resident or domestic student who meets one of the eligibility criteria for full-time study set out by the Ministry of Education.

    Fee-paying

    A student who is not eligible under the Ministry of Education’s criteria for a funded place at Te Kura may be able to enrol as a fee-paying student. The exact fees depend on the student’s citizenship status, place of residence and level of study. Enrolments are for a calendar year only.

    Overseas

    An overseas student is aged between 5–19 years old, is a New Zealand citizen or a permanent resident (having resided in New Zealand for two years or more) and meets one of the full-time overseas eligibility criteria as set out by the Ministry of Education.

    Adult

    If you are turning 20 years during the year of enrolment you will be categorised as an adult student. You must be living in New Zealand and a New Zealand citizen, permanent resident or domestic student and not attending a secondary school full-time. You will pay an administration fee of $110 per course. Enrolments are for a calendar year only.

    Note: Adults enrolling in online courses must have access to a suitable device and internet connectivity, so check our course information pages to see whether your course is online before enrolling.

    Young adult

    A young adult student is aged 16–19 and is not attending a school full-time. Students enrolled as a young adult may remain enrolled up to and including the end of the school year in which the student turns 19 years of age. Use the 'adult' category to enrol online as a young adult student.
