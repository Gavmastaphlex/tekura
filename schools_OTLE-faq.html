    OTLE FAQ

    Where can I log into OTLE?

    Use the Login drop down menu on our homepage.

    How do school coordinators login to OTLE?

    Your username is your school’s Ministry of Education (MoE) identification number (e.g. 1234 or AE123). If you’ve logged into OTLE before you should use your existing password.

    If not, use the default password 12345678 the first time you login, then create your own unique password. Please contact your Relationship Coordinator if you need your MoE number.

    What do school coordinators have access to in OTLE?

    Your OTLE login provides full access to all Te Kura course materials. It also provides access to your OTLE help area (00-Dual Schools). School Coordinators have different access from that of students, in that you are able to see all the courses your students are registered for, along with the 00-Dual Schools course and information about your student’s use of OTLE via the Audit User function. Students have access only to the courses that they are currently registered for, along with the OTLEHUB course, which has useful tips and information for students.

    To access your 00-Dual Schools course after logging into OTLE, click on the down arrow next to ‘Select a course’ in the top menu bar and click on 00-Dual Schools.

    What do I do if I’m having issues with OTLE?

    The 00-Dual Schools and OTLEHUB courses have lots of useful tips and information. For navigation and functionality queries first go to the homepage in OTLE to view the ‘How to’ guide information and help videos.

    For technical issues or problems logging in, email helpdesk.OTLE@tekura.school.nz or phone 0800 65 99 88 ext 8712 between the hours of 8am and 5pm.

    How do students access OTLE?

    Students are sent their login information by email on registration of their enrolment.

    Each student’s user name and initial password will be their Te Kura student ID number, which will be included in the email with their login instructions. Students are required to set a unique password when they first log in. Students who have already set an OTLE password can continue to use that password in the following year.

    My student hasn’t received their login instructions – what can we do?

    If your student’s enrolment has been approved and they are listed on your Te Kura Enrolment System (TES) Registered Students screen, but they haven’t received an email with OTLE login instructions, they can log in by going to the TES login page. Each student’s username and initial password will be their Te Kura student ID number.

    Students will be required to set a unique password when they first log in.

    Coordinators and students can contact our helpdesk at helpdesk.OTLE@tekura.school.nz or phone 0800 65 99 88 ext 8712 during office hours if they have difficulties logging in.

    What if a student or coordinator forgets their password?

    The ‘forgot password’ button on the OTLE login page can be used to reset the password. An email will be sent to the address that was provided at the time of the student’s enrolment with a password reset link. The link is valid for 14 days.

    Do fee-paying/international students who attend face-to-face schools need to access their NCEA level 2 and 3 course materials online?

    Yes – fee-paying dual students are treated the same as other dual students. These students will be sent instructions to log into OTLE to access course materials and will have the same options for returning work as other dual students.
