    Fees

    There are no fees for students who meet the Ministry of Education’s enrolment criteria, including full-time students and young adults aged 16 to 19 who are not attending another school.

    Students aged 20 years and over pay $110 administration fee per course.

    Students aged under 16 years that do not meet the criteria are generally not eligible to enrol full-time at Te Kura as fee-paying students. Exceptions to the rules include:

    • students under 16 years of age with a Ministry of Education Certificate of Exemption to be home schooled
    • students aged 16 or older who are New Zealand citizens or permanent residents living overseas
    • international students or New Zealand citizens or permanent residents living in New Zealand enrolled at a school (in such cases, the school of enrolment must register the student and pay the applicable fee).

    See our information sheet to find out the subject fees for fee-paying students living in New Zealand or overseas.

    Want to know more?

    Check our FAQ section to see if your question is covered, or talk to one of our enrolment advisors at 0800 LEARN NOW (0800 532 766).
