    Certificate in teacher aiding

    Te Kura has a partnership with the Open Polytechnic, which enables supervisors of full-time and early childhood students to study towards a Certificate in Teacher Aiding for a significantly reduced fee.

    The programme offers you the opportunity to gain a deeper understanding of how to support your student’s learning and is a level 4, NZQA-accredited 40 credit programme.

    Practical requirements

    For supervisors of Years 1–8

    Your work as a Te Kura supervisor of students in Years 1–8 meets the practical requirements for the programme, although we suggest that you attend our event days in your area while you are studying. We also recommend you spend some time at a primary or intermediate school where you can observe and interact with groups of children.

    For supervisors of Year 9 and above

    If you are supervising students in Year 9 or above, you will need to complete the programme’s practical requirements through a primary or intermediate school.
