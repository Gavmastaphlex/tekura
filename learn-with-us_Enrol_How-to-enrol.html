    How to enrol

    Te Kura offers several types of enrolments and you may enrol at any time of year. To determine your eligibility, follow the steps in our eligibility guide [Being developed] or read the criteria set out in the Ministry of Education’s enrolment policy.

    Once you’ve confirmed your eligibility, you can enrol online or download [CONFIRM - Separate page to display forms? ] an enrolment form.

    You may need to submit documents to support your application for enrolment, depending on what category you are enrolling under (see ‘Application requirements’ below).

    If you are applying under Ministry of Education enrolment criteria, you must be able to give proof of New Zealand citizenship, permanent residence or verified domestic student status. This is not required if you have attended a school in New Zealand during the last five years.

    Application requirements

    Check out the categories below to work out:

    • what category to enrol under
    • what documents to provide.

    Overseas students

    All students living overseas who are New Zealand citizens or permanent residents need to send proof of citizenship status with their application. This includes fee-payers.

    If you are eligible under Ministry of Education requirements, we need a letter from your parents’/guardians’ employer. The letter must say:

    • there is no employee subsidy for education
    • the family will be returning to New Zealand
    • no suitable education is available locally.

    Geographic isolation

    If you are applying under the geographic isolation category, you may need to provide proof of citizenship. When we receive your application form, we will check your eligibility with the Ministry of Education School Transport Service Agent.

    Itinerancy

    If you are applying under the itinerancy category, please complete the itinerary on page 5 of the enrolment form, showing the dates and the locality of your movements over the next six months. Provide details of locations to be visited, dates and duration of stay.

    Elite athlete/dancer/musician/sports/performer

    For students applying under the elite athlete/dancer/musician/sports/performer category, you will need to provide a letter of attestation from the formally constituted and recognised national body for your sport or artistic form. It should say:

    • you are likely to represent New Zealand or be in the top grade for your age group
    • your programme is appropriate and prevents regular attendance at a regular school
    • how long you are going to be enrolled.

    The letter should include an educational profile from your previous school.

    Pregnant or primary caregiver

    If you are applying under the pregnant or primary caregiver category, we need a medical or midwife’s certificate on letterhead that gives the baby’s expected due date, or a copy of the baby’s birth certificate.

    Child Youth and Family custody

    If your student is under the custody of Child Youth and Family (CYF), you will need to provide a letter signed by the CYF practice or service delivery unit manager. It should state that the student is in CYF custody or guardianship, the likely length of enrolment and the supervision arrangements for study.

    Early childhood (medical grounds)

    For early childhood enrolment applications on medical grounds, please send a certificate from a specialist in the condition that is preventing the student’s attendance.

    Special developmental needs

    If your student has special developmental needs, we need a Ministry of Education Special Education report verifying that no local service can meet your student’s needs.

    Fee-payers living in New Zealand

    • For students aged between 6 and 15, send us a copy of your exemption to home school.
    • If you are a New Zealand citizen, permanent resident or verified domestic student, please send proof of your citizenship status. If you are not a New Zealand citizen, permanent resident or verified domestic student, and are not enrolled with another school, you cannot enrol with us.
    • If you are a current school student enrolling in an extra class which is unfunded, you need to send us a letter of support from your school principal.
