    Principal’s Nominees

    Our Service Level Agreement[Definition] (SLA) outlines the enrolling school’s responsibilities for entering dual students for NCEA and for reporting NCEA achieved standards to NZQA.

    Please read our Principal’s Nominee Handbook and refer to the list of achievement and unit standards[To confirm] to see the full range of standards that could be assessed for each course.

    You must use Te Kura’s provider code 498 when entering dual students for standards and for reporting achieved standards to NZQA.

    Important information

    Important information for schools – 2016 NZQA entries and results

    NCEA assessment information sheet for secondary dual students

    Internal assessment

    • If you are required to enter indicative internally assessed entries, please note many students may not be assessed for all standards within a course, or they may be offered additional standards.
    • Results for internally assessed standards are available through both the Te Kura Enrolment System (TES) Results screen[Definition] and the Reports tool. Principal nominees are provided with their own TES logon. Contact Enrolment Services for your school’s login details.
    • These internally assessed results must be entered on your school management system and submitted to NZQA, with the correct version number and using our provider code, 498.

    External assessment

    • You will need to enter the appropriate externally assessed standards onto your management system.
    • The Te Kura Coordinator in your school should help students identify which externally assessed standards they wish to enter and encourage them to discuss the selection with their Te Kura subject teacher.
    • All entries for external assessments must be submitted to NZQA with our provider code, 498.
